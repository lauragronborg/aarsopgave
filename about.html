WHAT WE ARE ALL ABOUT

We are a copenhagen-based design duo built upon a lifelong friendship and an everlasting love for people and bicyclism.

Larsen & eriksen is our take on a welcome-for-all universe evolving around a collection of watches inspired by the 50’s unique era of clean, timeless danish design - stir fried with a raw, urban twist of contemporary copenhagen.

As part of our #BIKESFORAHAPPIERWORLD initiative we are distributing abandoned bikes from denmark to farmers, doctors, teachers, moms, dads and children in senegal and the gambia. Every time we sell a watch, we donate an inner tube to one of these amazing (bicycling) humans.

WE CALL IT: TIME FOR EVERYONE. EVERYWHERE.
