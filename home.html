HEJ! / HELLO!

Welcome to the online world og Larsen & Eriksen.

We are a Copenhagen-based design duo built upon a lifelong friendship and an everlasting love for people and bicyclism.

As part of our #BIKESFORAHAPPIERWORLD initiative we are distributing abandoned bikes from Denmark to farmers, doctors, teachers, moms, dads and children in Senegal and the Gambia - and every time we sell a watch, we donate an inner tube to one of these amazing (bicycling) human beings.

We would love for you to tag along on our journey towards creating time for everyone. Everywhere.

Thanks a million for supporting.
